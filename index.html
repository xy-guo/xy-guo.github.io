Xiaoyang Guo (郭晓阳)

I did my PhD at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I was advised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Hongsheng Li. Before that, I got my bachelor degree of Computer Science at Tsinghua University.

Now I am a researcher at Huawei Riemann Lab, mainly working on large-scene 3D reconstruction and neural rendering. My research interests focus on 3D computer vision, particularly on 3D perception, reconstruction, and rendering (NeRF).
