Xiaoyang Guo (郭晓阳)

I am currently a principal researcher at Horizon Robotics, learning a small team on 3D reconstruction and simulation for autonomous driving system. I got my Ph.D. degree from Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I was advised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Hongsheng Li. Before that, I got my bachelor degree of Computer Science at Tsinghua University.

I'm intertested in 3D computer vision and generative AI, especially large-scale 3D reconstruction, rendering, and generation..

Looking for self-motivated interns on neural rendering and generative AI. Feel free to email me if you are interested.
