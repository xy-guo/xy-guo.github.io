Xiaoyang Guo

Computer Vision Researcher

The Chinese University of Hong Kong

Biography

Xiaoyang Guo got his PhD degree of Electronic Engineerning at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Hongsheng Li. He got his bachelor degree of Computer Science at Tsinghua University in 2017. His research mainly focuses on combining 3D geometry with deep learning.
